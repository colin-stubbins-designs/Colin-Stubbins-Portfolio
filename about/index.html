About Me

Colin Stubbins

Graphic Designer, Toronto ON

I graduated the Graphic Design program at Humber Polytechnic with honours. I am a creative strategist with a strong spatial awareness and a passion for art and design that I have had since childhood. I've always enjoyed drawing what I saw and would also create cartoon characters in the doodle books we would get in elementary school. I went through about six of those doodle books each year.

I always find myself analyzing design and patterns around me and can quickly spot inconsistencies and flaws. When I see these, I always think "How could I fix that if I helped work on that design?"

I am also very math oriented, which comes into play when I'm making design measurements in packaging. This also helps me to keep my layouts and files very organized.

I treat all my projects with love and care and will accept nothing less than exceptional results.
